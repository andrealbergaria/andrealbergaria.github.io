Powers have correspondence, to multiples...

 

Examples

 

3*3

 3+3+3 k=3

 

5*6

 6+6+6+6+6 k=5

 

 

3^2 = k=3

3^3 = k=9

3^4 = k=27

3^5 = k=81

 

 

2^2 = k=2
