Bolzano theorem

*** Used Chat gpt

Let f be a continuous function on the closed interval [a, b], and suppose that f(a) and f(b) have opposite signs; that is, f(a) × f(b) < 0. Then there exists at least one c in the open interval (a, b) such that f(c) = 0.
